Update 1.0.4 - ???

Additions:
Added the Gaia's Rose, a Mana Rose alternative with lingering petals that uses Jungle Rose in recipe instead.

Reworks:
Reworked Eye of Nove to be in-line with Eye of Magnus rework.
Changed Wulfrum Leech Dagger to be almost normal shortsword.

Removals:
Removed Calamity's Mana Rose recipe that uses Jungle Rose.

Balance:
Nerfed the damage of the Depths Echo Staff from 40 to 28.
Nerfed the damage of the Hellstone Shellfish Staff from 200 to 185.
Buffed the damage of Auric Kunai from 140 to 145.
Adjusted Auric Kunai's use time from 1 to 3, use animation from 10 to 9, and removed reuse delay.
Pyro Stone now inflicts Brimstone Flames for 1 second instead of Dragonfire for half a second.
Rebalanced the damage of the Eye of Nova Staff from 400 to 500 and attack speed from from 10 to 15 ticks.
Removed hidden speed buff to Flame Cube compared to Exo Box.
Removed speed nerf to Plague Station and changed to be skin variant of Exo Box.
Plague Station no longer drops from The Plaguebringer Goliath and now obtaned by crafting it with an Exo Box.
Frozen Armor's parry now negates all damage instead of reducing damage to 1.

Changes:
Renamed Pearl of Fish Calamity to Treasure of Clamity.
Renamed Flame Cube to Exo Box (Calamity Edition) and updated tooltip.
Renamed Plague Station to Exo Box (Plague Edition) and updated tooltip.
Renamed Wither Bane to Wither Storm.
Updated Soul Baguette & Exo Baguette to match the changes done to the base Baguette in Calamity 2.1.
Rewrote the Clamitas & Wall of Bronze lore items.

Fixes:
Fixed some of the tooltips to match the new format of Calamity tooltips.
Fixed a bug that caused The Subcommunity to gain a negative mining boost.
Fixed a bug that caused the Wulfrum Lure to fire bobbers without charge or expending Wulfrum Scrap.
Fixed Auric Kunai still requiring 100 Lunar Kunai's in it's recipe.
Fixed Auric Kunai not being able to generate stealth.
Fixed Titan Scale Potion buff effect not resets after removing buff.
Centain localization fixes (expecialy russian).
